Stephen Curry's career.

Stephen Curry is the point-guard of the Golden State Warriors who led them to the first championship since the 1900's. He is the fifth highest paid player in his team and is one of the best player in the NBA. He has a wife and the father of one daughter. He is the son of the NBA player Dell Curry and brother to Seth Curry.
